# Security Policy

Bifract is a log management platform that may house sensitive data. Security issues are taken seriously.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Use [GitHub's private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/zaneGittins/bifract/security/advisories/new) to submit a report.

Include what you found, how to reproduce it, and the potential impact. Reports must include a working proof of concept or clear reproduction steps. Automated scanner output or AI-generated reports without demonstrated impact will be closed without review.
